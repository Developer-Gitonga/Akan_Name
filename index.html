Akan-Name Calculator

Hey folks! Most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born.
Luckily for us, this web app lets us calculate the day of the week from a specific date. According to your calculated day, you will be assigned an Akan name derived from Ghanaian culture.

First input your birth date. Then gender.
Finally you will receive your Akan names.
